What this is about

Rash driving is a significant traffic violation in most states. Rash driving is defined as driving with wilful disregard for the safety of people or property. When convicted of reckless driving, the driver can face severe penalties such as fines, license suspension, and even jail time. The intuition of this project is to enhance the performance of driving risk analysis by enlarging the influential factors set and finding a sophisticated analysing method

How it works

This product records all driving data and upload it to the cloud, from where it willl analysed and processed using machine learning algorithms. Using GPS Module, current position and velocity of the vehicle will be calculated, using the velocity, distance calculations sudden speed Variations can be detected. Close calls of the vehicle can be detected by using sensors and the distance between them is also calculated to analyse whether the vehicle is rash driving and overtaking vehicles. The raw data obtained from the vehicle module is used to classify whether the incident occurred is to be considered as rash driving or not using logistic regression. A clear analysis of the driver about rash driving can be viewed from the Mobile Application.
